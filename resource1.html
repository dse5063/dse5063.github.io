Penn State Mechanical and Nuclear
Department Website
www.mne.psu.edu

The Penn State Mechanical and Nuclear Engineering Department website is an online resource area for anyone associated with the MNE department. This is the most valuable resource for all undergraduate Nuclear engineering majors. The site provides news and upcoming events for the department on the main page. On other pages, research, student and alumni resources, and a database of staff can be found and is open to everyone. Nuclear engineering students are able to find faculty who perform research in the nuclear field and then get in contact to see what kind of help is needed for the research. Also, under the Student tabs, there is a "Current Students" area. This area has information for advising, academic plans, courses, etc. There is information on different clubs to join that will help further your education in the nuclear field.

User Tips:

  • On the home page, use the search bar up in the top right of the page to search for material you cannot find easily yourself.
  • For undergraduate students, mainly use the "students" tab for the most information on what should be needed while attending Penn State.
