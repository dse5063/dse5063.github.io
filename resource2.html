American Nuclear Society
www.ans.org

The American Nuclear Society is a professional organization for the nuclear field. The website contains information on joining ANS, upcoming meetings, scientific publications, career help, and much more. Becoming a member of ANS is not mandatory. However, joining will allow you to go to events, meet more individuals in the nuclear field, and search through the extensive amount of publications. Nuclear students can find nuclear standards, publications, and career advice easily through the navigation bar. The ANS store has a lot of the books used during an undergraduates time in university and gives discounts to members.

User Tips:

  • At the bottom of the home page, you can find categories and links to basically everything on the site.
  • The search bar is great way to find something that might not be specifically listed, like a publication name.
