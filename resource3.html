Engineering Toolbox
www.engineeringtoolbox.com

Engineering Toolbox is a website specifically tailored to basic information and tools for engineering and design of technical applications. The main topics can be found listed down the middle, with a description, or on the left of the page. There is also a unit converter on the left that helps. Almost all of the equations and properties needed for undergraduate success in engineering can be found on this site. When navigating through the site, each topic has a description of what it is and how to use it. In the first two years of engineering, many of these topics will be learned and so a different approach can be found on this website. In the final two years of nuclear engineering, students will still need to know thermodynamics, heat transfer, material properties, etc. So this site is helpful for being a collection of almost all things needed in the engineering curriculum.

User Tips:

  • The search bar is not that great, looking on the main page for the specific criteria needed is usually easier.
  • It is hard to see but on the upper left there are small icons that link to pages for 3D and 2D schematic help.
