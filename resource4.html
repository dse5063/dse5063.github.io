Chegg
www.chegg.com

Chegg is a database of textbook solutions, questions, and tutors. There is a subscription fee needed to find solutions and use tutors, but it can be worth it. Books can also be found on Chegg and might be a bit cheaper than other sites. Chegg Study offers the solutions and Q&A, which is a great idea if you really need help. Undergrads in nuclear engineering can find this to be helpful in non-nuclear classes and to help overall grades in classes. Solutions can be used to teach topics and a Q&A can help on other topics not in Chegg already.

User Tips:

  • If you use Chegg for homework help, cite it in your work.
  • When looking at solutions/questions look at the rating it is given. Sometimes the solution is wrong.
