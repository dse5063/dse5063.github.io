Nukeworker.com
www.nukeworker.com

Nukeworker.com is a site mainly used for it's forum page. On the main page, job listings, news, training help, etc. can be found. The forum can get you in touch with professionals in the nuclear field and find information on all sides of the industry. Nuclear engineering students can use this for asking questions about radiation and reactors. If going for a job in a power plant, students can find practice exams and discussions about the exams. There are many Penn State graduates on the forum page, which can offer advice and tips for future endeavors.

User Tips:

  • On the main page, the forums page can be found under the forum tab on the navigation bar.
