Nuclear-Power.net
www.nuclear-power.net

Nuclear-Power.net is a website built by engineers to help the public learn about nuclear energy. The website has easy mobility with the navigation bar with the main topics. For undergrads looking to get ahead before getting into the prime nuclear courses at Penn State, this website offers information for every level of knowledge on the subject. The basics of nuclear power plants and reactions are prime for courses at Penn State.

User Tips:

  • On the main page, under the about section, there is a choose your topic section that looks like the navigation bar at the top, but takes you right to the basic level of each topic.
  • As you hover over the topics in the navigation bar, a more concise level of topics shows and can make finding something more specific faster.
