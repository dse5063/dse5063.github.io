Nuclear Science and Engineering Journal
www.ans.tandfonline.com/toc/unse20/current

The Nuclear Science and Engineering Journal is a collection of publications written by professionals in the nuclear field. The journal is part of the American Nuclear Society (ANS). There are currently 189 volumes in the collection, which include multiple articles in 3 issues of each volume. Each publication is reviewed by other professionals in the nuclear field. Nuclear Engineering undergraduate students can use this journal to find material for research purposes or just to understand other areas of nuclear science.

User Tips:

  • If looking for a specific subject, use the search bar. Otherwise, it may take you a while to find something relevant.
  • You can use the citation search to look up publications from citations in other publications using the volume number, issue, and start page.
